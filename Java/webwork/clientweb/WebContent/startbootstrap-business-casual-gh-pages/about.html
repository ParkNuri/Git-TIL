In November, the season that reminds us a cup of warm coffee, the best coffee festival “Seoul Coffee Festival” will fill Seoul with the aroma of Coffee!

- Date : Nov. 7(Thu) ~ 10(Sun), 2019, 4 days
- Location : The lobby of the East Gate, 1st floor, Coex
- 2019 Theme Trip to your own COFFEE LAND! Take a trip to your own coffee land this November at the 4th Seoul Coffee Festival! This year’s theme of Seoul Coffee Festival is COFFEE LAND, where you can take a journey to explore and discover your taste in coffee! As being coffee-lander! Let’s become a Coffee-Lander traveling to find your own value of coffee with the various of programs
- Program ZONE 1. FOR ME
– A journey to finding your own coffee (Coffee Training Station, Coffee Alley, Seoul Coffee Tour Bus)
ZONE 2. FOR COFFEE
– A journey made by, for and with coffee (Coffee Science Lab, Coffee Art Gallery)
ZONE 3. FOR US
– A journey about the story about coffee & me, and all of us.
